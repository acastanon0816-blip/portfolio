Five Color Themes

I created the themes below in a palette form in Photoshop. Each theme represents a design type that I could use in various rooms in my home, including the homeschool space for my daughter. I also share the mood each palette communicates.

Theme Name Scheme Type Best For Palette Image Explanation
Soothing Coastal Analogous (cool) Bathroom, Spa, Wellness, Health Cool blues/greens create a calm, trustworthy mood and support readability.
Dynamic Contrast Complementary Modern Home Office, Ads Strong opposites create energy and draw attention to key content.
Warm & Inviting Warm palette Community, family brands Warm tones feel friendly and inviting while still allowing hierarchy.
Minimally Modern Neutral + accent Kitchens, Bathrooms, Professional portfolios Neutrals keep the design clean; one accent color adds emphasis. For kitchens or bathrooms a metallic accent adds another texture.
Primary Fun Triadic Classroom, Play area, Kids brands, learning Bright balanced colors create a fun tone while staying structured.
